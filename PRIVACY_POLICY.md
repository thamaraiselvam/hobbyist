# Privacy Policy for Hobbyist

**Last Updated:** February 1, 2026

## Introduction

Hobbyist ("we", "our", or "the app") is a hobby tracking application that helps users build consistent habits through daily completion tracking. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

## Data Collection and Usage

### Data Stored Locally on Your Device

The following data is stored **only on your device** using SQLite database and is **never transmitted to our servers**:

- **Hobby Information**: Hobby names, descriptions, notes, colors, priorities, and repeat modes
- **Completion Records**: All your daily completion data and streaks
- **User Preferences**: App settings, display name, notification preferences
- **Sound Effects**: Completion sound settings

This data remains completely private and under your control. Uninstalling the app will permanently delete all local data.

### Data Collected and Transmitted

When you use Hobbyist, we collect limited data through Firebase services to improve app functionality and performance:

#### 1. Google Sign-In (Optional)
- **What we collect**: Email address, name, profile picture URL, and Google user ID
- **Purpose**: User authentication and personalization
- **Note**: Sign-in is completely optional. The app works fully offline without authentication.

#### 2. Analytics Data (Firebase Analytics)
- **What we collect**:
  - Screen views and navigation patterns
  - Feature usage statistics (e.g., "hobby created", "notification scheduled")
  - App version and device type
  - Country/region (approximate location)
- **What we DO NOT collect**: Hobby names, notes, completion data, or any personal hobby information
- **Purpose**: Understand how users interact with the app to improve features

#### 3. Crash Reports (Firebase Crashlytics)
- **What we collect**:
  - Stack traces and crash logs
  - Device model, OS version, app version
  - Crash timestamp and app state
- **What we DO NOT collect**: Hobby data or personal information from crash reports
- **Purpose**: Identify and fix app crashes to improve stability

#### 4. Performance Monitoring (Firebase Performance)
- **What we collect**:
  - App startup time
  - Screen rendering performance
  - Network request latency (if applicable)
- **Purpose**: Optimize app performance

#### 5. Remote Configuration (Firebase Remote Config)
- **What we collect**: Device-level configuration requests (no personal data)
- **Purpose**: Enable/disable features remotely and conduct A/B testing

### Notification Data
- Local notifications are scheduled on your device only
- No notification content or scheduling data is transmitted externally
- Requires device notification permission

## Third-Party Services

Hobbyist uses the following third-party services:

### Google Firebase
- **Services Used**: Authentication, Analytics, Crashlytics, Performance Monitoring, Remote Config
- **Privacy Policy**: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy
- **Data Processing**: Data is processed according to Google's privacy practices

### Google Sign-In
- **Privacy Policy**: https://policies.google.com/privacy
- **Usage**: Optional authentication only

## Data Sharing

We do **NOT**:
- Sell your data to third parties
- Share your hobby information with anyone
- Use your data for advertising purposes
- Track you across other apps or websites

We **ONLY** share anonymized analytics and crash data with Google Firebase for the purposes stated above.

## Your Rights and Choices

### Account Deletion
If you signed in with Google:
1. Go to Settings in the app
2. Select "Sign Out" to disconnect your account
3. Uninstall the app to delete all local data

### Data Export
All your data is stored locally on your device. To export:
- Contact us for technical guidance on accessing your SQLite database
- We do not store your data on our servers, so there is nothing to export from us

### Opt-Out of Analytics
Currently, analytics are collected by default. Future versions may include an opt-out option in Settings.

## Data Security

- All local data is stored in a secure SQLite database on your device
- Firebase connections use industry-standard encryption (HTTPS)
- We do not have access to your local hobby data
- No backend server stores your personal information

## Children's Privacy

Hobbyist does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you are a parent and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be posted with a new "Last Updated" date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

## Data Retention

- **Local Data**: Retained until you uninstall the app or manually delete hobbies
- **Firebase Data**: Retained according to Firebase/Google policies (typically 2-14 months for analytics, indefinitely for crash logs unless manually deleted)

## Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

For users in the European Economic Area (EEA):
- **Consent**: Google Sign-In requires explicit consent
- **Legitimate Interest**: Analytics and crash reporting to improve app functionality
- **Right to Access**: Contact us to request information about data collected
- **Right to Deletion**: Uninstall the app and sign out of Google to remove data

## California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

California residents have the right to:
- Know what personal information is collected
- Request deletion of personal information
- Opt-out of the sale of personal information (we do not sell data)

Contact us to exercise these rights.
